Small text

Use small text for metainformation, less important notices or other secondary stuff.

Weak text

This dimmed or "weak" text and can be used for non-essential information.

You can even make the weak text small and let it stand out even less. The copyright notice is set with these styles (see the bottom of this page).

Monospaced text

Monospaced text is suitable for serial numbers, code, or other things that require an extra high level of legibility. Here the aesthetics are a secondary concern.

Highlighted text

Use this style to highlight words or sentences in a body of text.
